Projects in Progress

Planned Projects

                    short papers on topics they find interesting. I would like to include an online journal on the site so that students who would like to later go into academia can get a 
                    taste of what this world is roughly like (at least the publishing and research parts) to get them a clearer picture whether or not it's right for them.
